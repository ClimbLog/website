Delete Your Account

Account deletion is handled directly in the ClimbLog app.

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All of your flight logs, settings, and personal data will be removed from our systems.

How to delete your account
  1. 1
    Open ClimbLog Launch the app on your iOS or Android device.
  2. 2
    Go to Settings Tap the profile icon or navigate to the Settings menu.
  3. 3
    Select "Account" Find the Account section within your settings.
  4. 4
    Tap "Delete Account" Confirm your decision when prompted to permanently delete your account and all associated data.

Need help? If you're having trouble accessing your account or need assistance with the deletion process, please review our Privacy Policy or contact KeyQueue LLC for support.
