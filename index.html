Features

Everything a pilot needs

Built for student pilots and seasoned aviators alike. Simple to use, powerful when you need it.

Quick Flight Entry

Log a flight in seconds. Enter departure and arrival airports, aircraft, date, and flight time — no paperwork required.

Hour Tracking & Totals

Automatically totals PIC, SIC, dual, night, instrument, cross-country, and more — always up to date.

Flight History

Browse your complete logbook with powerful filtering by aircraft, airport, date range, or flight type.

Logbook Export

Export your logbook to PDF for checkrides, medical exams, or insurance — formatted to FAA standards.

Secure Cloud Sync

Your logbook is safely backed up to the cloud and syncs across all your devices automatically.

Currency Tracking

Stay aware of your recency requirements. ClimbLog tracks landings, IFR currency, and more so nothing slips.
